Welcome to Jardins Sans Secret!

You can now create your online store within a few minutes in our gardening marketplace.

The steps are extremely easy: 

★ Update the Welcome, About Us, Terms & Conditions and Shipping Policy pages to create your online store profile. This profile can be changed at any time as we want you to have full control over how your store looks.

★ Select the option Product Management => Import products from CSV to upload your product catalog to our database. Please note that only comma's-delimited text files (.csv) are accepted. 

You are done! We will start displaying your products in our related articles, enabling consumers to shop directly from your website (allow 24 hours for your online store to be updated).  

★ Please remember to visit us regularly to update your product listings to ensure an up-to-date offering.

If you are unsure how to proceed or need assistance, we will be more than happy to help. You can contact us at any moment (or almost!) at this email address: info@jardins-sans-secret.com

Thank you for joining us and good luck!
